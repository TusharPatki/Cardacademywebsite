Reward Structure

Base Rewards

  • 1% cashback on all eligible online and offline spends

Accelerated Rewards

  • 5% cashback on transactions with 10 partner online merchants:
    • Amazon
    • BookMyShow
    • Cult.fit
    • Flipkart
    • Myntra
    • Sony LIV
    • Swiggy
    • Tata CLiQ
    • Uber
    • Zomato

Reward Caps

  • CashPoints earned under the 5% category are capped at 1,000 CashPoints per statement cycle
  • CashPoints earned under the 1% category are also capped at 1,000 CashPoints per statement cycle
  • Maximum of 2,000 CashPoints can be earned in a given statement cycle

CashPoint Value

  • 1 CashPoint = ₹1 when redeemed for cashback
  • 1 CashPoint = ₹0.30 when redeemed for product catalog or flights/hotels on SmartBuy
  • 1 CashPoint = 0.30 AirMiles when converted to airline miles

Exclusions

CashPoints are not earned on:

  • Fuel purchases
  • Rent payments
  • Government-related transactions
  • EMI transactions
  • Wallet loading
  • Cash advances
  • Payment of card fees
  • Smart EMI/Dial an EMI transactions

This reward structure makes the HDFC Millennia Credit Card particularly attractive for frequent online shoppers who use the partner merchants regularly, while still offering decent rewards on other purchases

Redemption

  • Minimum 500 CashPoints required for redemption against card's statement balance
  • ₹50 fee for each cash against statement redemption request
  • CashPoints are valid for 2 years before expiring

Milestone Benefits

Who Should Apply?

The HDFC Infinia Credit Card is ideal for:

  1. High-net-worth individuals with significant spending power
  1. Frequent travelers who can benefit from unlimited lounge access and travel insurance
  1. Luxury shoppers and dining enthusiasts
  1. Those seeking premium concierge services and exclusive lifestyle benefits

When Not to Apply

  • If your annual spending is below ₹15 lakh, as the annual fees may outweigh the benefits
  • If you rarely travel or don't utilize premium dining and lifestyle perks
  • If you prefer straightforward cashback rewards over point-based systems

Tips to Maximize Benefits

  1. Utilize SmartBuy for travel bookings to earn 10X reward points
  1. Take advantage of the complimentary golf games and Club Marriott membership
  1. Use the card for international transactions to benefit from the low 2% forex markup
  1. Aim to meet the ₹10 lakh annual spend threshold to waive the annual fee
